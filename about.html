The Man Behind the Blog

Manjunathswamy Hiremath

I'm a Post Graduate in Agriculture Sciences and Research Scholar who spent 8 enriching years working in Public Sector Banking before transitioning into the world of financial markets. For the past 4 years, I've been an active trader — primarily in options and equities on Indian exchanges.

My background in agriculture gave me a deep understanding of commodity cycles, seasonal patterns, and ground-level economic realities that most city-based traders miss. My banking years taught me credit risk, financial analysis, and the patience that institutional trading demands. Together, these form the foundation of how I approach markets.

Beyond markets, I'm a voracious reader, a weekend traveller who finds Karnataka's heritage endlessly fascinating, and someone who believes that good food and clear thinking go hand-in-hand. This blog is where all four worlds collide.
